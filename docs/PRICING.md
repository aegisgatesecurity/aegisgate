## Frequently Asked Questions

### General

**Q: Can I change plans later?**
> Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, and downgrades apply at the start of your next billing cycle.

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
> We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and wire transfers for Enterprise plans.

**Q: Is there a free trial for paid plans?**
> Yes! Developer and Professional plans come with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start.

---

### Technical

**Q: What happens if I exceed my plan limits?**
> We'll notify you when you reach 80% of any limit. If you exceed limits, we'll work with you to upgrade your plan.

**Q: Can I run AegisGate on my own infrastructure?**
> Yes! Docker is available on all plans. Professional and Enterprise plans include Kubernetes support, and Enterprise supports on-premise deployment.

**Q: Do you offer self-hosted licensing?**
> Yes! All paid plans include self-hosted license validation. No data leaves your infrastructure.

---

### Billing

**Q: What's included in the price?**
> All plans include core functionality, updates, and security patches. Enterprise includes dedicated support and custom development.

**Q: Do you offer discounts for non-profits?**
> Yes! Contact us for special pricing for non-profits and startups with valid VC funding.

**Q: How does annual billing work?**
> Annual billing gives you 2 months free (approximately 17% savings). You can switch from monthly to annual at any time.
